ABOUT

I was born and raised in Detroit, MI, and being one of six girls, everything was competitive in our household. From video games, to school and sports, my desire to be the best was ingrained in me at a very young age. This drive helped me to excel academically and in basketball.

I was recruited to play basketball by many schools, and decided to attend the United States Military Academy. I graduated from West Point in 2010. While I was a cadet, I was faced with many challenges. Through them all, I learned that with hard work and dedication, there was nothing I couldn't accomplish.


I've worked in a variety of leadership positions before pursuing my passion in web design and development. I am currently based in Ann Arbor, MI working as a UX Designer. Contact me now so I can bring your next project to life!
